Who we are

The ICONIC Think Tank is a group of brilliant minds who came together to overcome the challenges of E-LAB and the world as whole. We are crazy, fun, creative, and hard working. Our mission is to lend a helping hand in the fight of unemployment especially in the youth by giving them IT employable skills, such as web development, machine learning, among others.

Challenges Solved

Challenge I

ALU EXPRESS
This was the first challenge, it was about solving riddles and the winners got 1500 points. The aim of this was to initiate us to think tank challenges and of course getting to know our campus. We got 800/1500 on this challenge

Challenge II

INTRODUCTION
Who are you? What drives you?. This was about each of us introducing ourselves in a 30 seconds pitch talking about what we enjoy doing, what we passionate about among others. We got 1700/1700 on this challenge.

Challenge III

DISCOVER AFRICA
This was about creating a short story about any African country highlighting a particular area and describing how it will look like in 35 years, for our think tank, we choose Mauritius and talked about its tourism sector. We got 1350/1350 on this challenge.

Challenge IV

COMMUNITY ENGAGEMENT
This was all about advocacy. We created a slide deck launching campaign against Child Malnutrition in our community. The slides can be found @ here.

Challenge V

$1 IMPACT
We were given $1= 1500 rwf to work on our mission. We used this to create a website that will impart IT skills to the youth. We believe by making these skills accessible we will lend a helping hand towards fighting unemployment.

Challenge VI

DIGITAL PRINT
This is all about creating a website to illustrate our Elab journey, which is the one you are viewing now :).
